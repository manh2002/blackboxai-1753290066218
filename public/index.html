The Leading Reinsurer in Vietnam

Hanoi Re (formerly known as PVI Re), established in 2011, is one of the leading reinsurance companies in Vietnam. Our reputation as a professional and reliable partner is built on our commitment to delivering superior insurance solutions and risk engineering services to our valued customers.

Our Products

Facultative Reinsurance

Hanoi Re offers comprehensive, tailored insurance solutions based on market expertise. With end-to-end support, we ensure solutions align with clients' financial capacities and meets clients' specific needs.

Treaty Reinsurance

Hanoi Re provides a broad spectrum of treaty reinsurance coverage on both proportional and non-proportional basis. As a trusted reinsurance provider in Vietnam, we prioritize safety and cost-effectiveness.

Risk Engineering Services

With our extensive knowledge of the local and international markets, Hanoi Re prioritizes delivering diverse value-added solutions to our clients with comprehensive risk management.
